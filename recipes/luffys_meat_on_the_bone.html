Luffy's Meat on the Bone

Join Luffy's feast: Savour the Meat on the Bone dish!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Drumsticks: 4
  • Hard-Boiled Eggs: 4
  • Breadcrumbs: 1/4 cup
  • Milk: 3 tbsp.

Mix

  • Ground Chicken: 18 oz. (500g)
  • Salt: 1 tsp.
  • Black Pepper: a little
  • Egg: 1
  • Vegetable Oil: As needed

Instructions

  1. Make chicken drumstick “tulips.” Use kitchen scissors to cut the meat loose from the handle end. Roll the meat down until inside-out at the end.
  2. Soak breadcrumbs in milk. Knead mix in a bowl, then add breadcrumbs and knead again.
  3. Fold meaty end of drumstick around a hard-boiled egg. If meat doesn't cover, add cuts to loosen. Oil hands and cover drumstick and egg with breadcrumb mixture.
  4. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15–20 minutes, watching carefully.
